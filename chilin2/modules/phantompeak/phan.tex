\section*{Fragment Length QC}
\subsection*{Strand Cross Correlation Figure}
In order to estimate the fragment length, we compute the strand cross-
correlation values against given shift-sizes.  The red-line(s) indicate
the shift-size(s) that result in the maximum correlation value, i.e.
the ``estimated'' fragment length.  The blue line represents the fragment
length that was used by MACS, i.e. the ``actual'' fragment length.  The
closer the two lines are, the better.
